This is my personal website. I've poured my heart into it over the past two months, working almost every day since I arrived at Hopeworks. It's both my pride and joy and my biggest challenge—I've spent so much time on it, but it's sadly is still a work in progress.


The central idea of my website revolves around games, game design and development, and the skills you can learn from creating your own games. I also touch on how these skills can be applied in professional fields.


On the homepage, you'll find a popular game showcased with a hook that I hope grabs your attention. If you scroll down, there's an animation featuring a list of indie games created by people just like us.


The second page, Dev Games, aims to show that there's no barrier to entry for learning how to make something. With the internet and the wealth of accessible information, there's never been a better time to pick up a new skill.


The third page, Skills, highlights how the abilities you develop through game creation can be transferred to other professional fields. I've chosen to focus on Programming, Sound Design, and Graphic Design.


The final page is the About Me page. This section is all about me—what I've worked on and a little insight into who I am. It's a space for me to imgsent myself and share my journey.
